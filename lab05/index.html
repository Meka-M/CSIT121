Reflection for Lab 5

When it comes to designing web pages, there are many design practices that are important to follow. HTML is the bones of a website, but the layout is just as important. Accessibility and responsiveness are two important things that a website has to have. This week we learned about flexbox and grid layout. These are responsive layouts and makes the website more appealing to look at. You can also use media queries to the site, which can change the sites screen from desktop screen sized to phone screen sized or tablet screen sized.
